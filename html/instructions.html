QuizApp - Instructions

Welcome to QuizApp!

Before you start the quiz, please take a moment to read the instructions below:

How to Play

  1. Click on the "Start Quiz" button to begin.
  2. Read each question carefully and select the correct answer.
  3. You can navigate between questions using the "Next" and "Previous" buttons.
  4. Once you've answered all the questions, click on the "Submit" button to see your results.

Scoring

Your score will be calculated based on the number of correct answers. Each question carries equal weight.

Time Limit

Some quizzes may have a time limit. Keep an eye on the timer at the top of the page to manage your time effectively.

Feedback

After submitting the quiz, you will receive feedback on your performance. Take note of the correct answers for future reference.

Good Luck!

Now that you know how to play, it's time to showcase your knowledge. Best of luck on the quiz!
